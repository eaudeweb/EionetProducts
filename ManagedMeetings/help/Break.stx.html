Meeting - Break Meeting - Break

.

Description

After defining the seesions one should define the Meeting Breaks. Example breaks: breakfast, morning
coffee, lunch and afternoon coffee. In this case, there would be four sessions in between.

*Note*: There is no time overlapping check for sessions and breaks and a session goes until a break is defined. It is in your job to syncronize them.

Controls

'Id' -- The id of the break.

'Title' -- The title of the break.

'Start/End time' -- Enter the start/end times in YYYY/MM/DD YY:MM format.

'Cost' -- Unused at present.
